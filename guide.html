Installation & Setup

Step 1: Add to Chrome

Go to the Chrome Web Store and search for 'TransVoice', then click the "Add to Chrome" button. Confirm by clicking "Add Extension".

Step 2: Pin the Extension (Important!)

Click the Puzzle icon on the top right of your browser. Find 'TransVoice AI' and click the Pin icon. This makes it easy to access.

Step 3: Login

Click the TransVoice icon. Sign in using your Email account to activate your free trial.

How to Start Translating

For Videos (YouTube or Any Website)

  1. Open the video tab.
  2. Click the TransVoice Extension icon.
  3. Select Browser Tab Option
  4. Choose Target Language (e.g., Spanish).
  5. Select "Voice Style" mode.
  6. Click Start Translation.

For Meetings (User-to-User)

  1. Open your transVoice extension.
  2. Select "Talk Voice" to hear other user's translated voice.
  3. Input "Partner email ID"
  4. Choose "Voice Style" mode.
  5. Choose Language "Important" Option: Select From field as your partner language. And select To field in your own language.
  6. To allow your microphone "Right click your extension Icon" and select Manage Extension, than "Allow" your microphone.
  7. Click Start Translation.

Pro Tip: Long Silence?

If the translated voice stops for 10-15 seconds while the video is running, simply Pause the video for 1-2 seconds and then Play it again.

Why this happens?
This occurs when the speaker talks continuously without taking a breath. The AI system waits for the sentence to complete to ensure the translation is grammatically correct.

Frequently Asked Questions

TransVoice AI is offer free minutes for test use?
Yes! We offer a free tier that gives you 3 minutes of translation credit. For heavy usage, you can upgrade to our affordable premium plans.
Is it suitable for Netflix or Movies?
Not Recommended. TransVoice AI is not optimized for movies or TV shows (like Netflix) where there is heavy background music, sound effects, or overlapping dialogue.

It is specifically developed for Descriptive & Educational content like Tutorials, E-learning courses, Documentaries, and News where the speech is clear.
Why is there no sound?
Please ensure you have selected the correct "Source" (System Audio vs Mic). Also, check if the tab is muted. If the problem persists, refresh the page and try again.
