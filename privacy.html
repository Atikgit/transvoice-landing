Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to TransVoice AI. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Chrome Extension and services.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and name (via Google/Microsoft Auth).
  • Audio Data: We process audio from your browser tab solely for the purpose of real-time translation. We do not store this audio permanently.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous metrics to improve extension performance.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Provide real-time translation services.
  • Manage your subscription and account balance.
  • Send important updates regarding the service.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with trusted third-party services necessary for operation (e.g., Firebase for authentication, LemonSqueezy for payments, Microsoft Azure for speech processing).

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at support@transvoice.ai.
