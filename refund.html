Refund Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. General Policy

Since TransVoice AI offers digital goods and real-time services (minutes of translation), all payments are generally non-refundable once the service has been used or minutes have been credited to your account.

2. Exceptions

We may consider a refund request under the following exceptional circumstances:

  • If you were charged due to a technical error on our end.
  • If the service was completely inaccessible to you for a prolonged period due to our server downtime.
  • If you request a refund within 24 hours of purchase AND have not used any translation minutes.

3. Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time. Your account will remain active until the end of the current billing cycle. No partial refunds will be issued for unused time in the billing cycle.

4. How to Request a Refund

If you believe you are eligible for a refund, please contact our support team at info@transvoice.ai with your email address, which is used for the account. We review requests on a case-by-case basis.
